Texas Monthly Has a New Logo

August 23rd, 2012  Design, Link

I know everyone’s talking about Microsoft’s new branding today, but I rather enjoyed this breakdown of Texas Monthly’s new logo.

We brought back the fatter ball terminals and added very small ball terminals to the serif of the ‘e’ and the lowercase ‘t.’ We also brought back the curve at the top of the lowercase ‘t.’ The differences may seem minor, but to me, it’s the details that complete the experience.

-TJ Tucker Texas Monthly Creative Director

Austinist had a little fun with the changes, but they look pretty stark if you ask me. Love that lowercase ‘t’ and ‘y.’ Details, details, details.
