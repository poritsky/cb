Music From & Inspired by Tiny Furniture

February 22nd, 2012  Link, Movies

In 2010, I wrote and recorded the score for Lena Dunham’s feature TINY FURNITURE. This release is the entire score, plus many other pieces I wrote for the film that didn’t make the cut, including my cover of Sinead O’Connor’s “Emperor’s New Clothes,” which was originally supposed to be the song over the end titles (we couldn’t afford the rights), and “When You Come Home,” my duet with Rebecca Schiffman that ended up being at the end instead.
